About us

Welcome to Neal P's Smoothie shop, where vibrant flavors and healthy living come together in the heart of Malibu, California. Nestled in this picturesque coastal town, our smoothie shop is dedicated to offering refreshing, nutritious, and delicious drinks made from the finest ingredients. Whether you're a local or visiting the area, we're here to fuel your day with a burst of natural energy and wholesome goodness.

At Neal P's Smoothie Shop, we offer four signature smoothies that are crafted to delight your taste buds while nourishing your body. Our Mango Smoothie is a tropical paradise in a cup, combining the natural sweetness of ripe mangoes with a creamy texture that's full of vitamins and antioxidants. For a refreshing and detoxifying option, the Celery Smoothie is light, crisp, and naturally hydrating, providing the perfect balance of wellness in every sip. Our Strawberry Blueberry Smoothie blends juicy strawberries with antioxidant-rich blueberries, creating a fruity, nutrient-packed delight that’s both delicious and good for you. And for a classic favorite, the Strawberry Banana Smoothie offers a smooth, creamy combination of sweet strawberries and ripe bananas that will leave you satisfied and refreshed.
